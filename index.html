About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a FrontEnd Developer with a deep passion for coding and gaming, where creativity meets technology. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) with a focus on Systems Development at Phinma Araullo University. This academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in programming languages, web development frameworks, and software engineering principles.

My fascination with technology began at a young age, and over the years, I have honed my skills in various front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I thrive on the challenge of transforming complex ideas into user-friendly interfaces that enhance the overall user experience. My portfolio showcases a variety of projects that reflect my commitment to quality and innovation.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I actively seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skill set through self study.

When I'm not coding, you can find me immersed in the world of gaming, which fuels my creativity and problem-solving skills. I believe that gaming and programming share a unique synergy, as both require strategic thinking, attention to detail, and a passion for storytelling. I often draw inspiration from my gaming experiences to create engaging and interactive web applications.

As I continue my journey in the tech industry, I am excited about the endless possibilities that lie ahead. I am eager to take on new challenges, collaborate with innovative minds, and contribute to projects that make a positive impact. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you!
